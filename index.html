Bling Runner

Instructions

TL;DR: Catch the blings!*

To advance levels, you must grab enough blings. If you don't, game over. How many blings, you may ask? As many as you can! Every bling you grab increases your acceleration. Trust me here.. you'll need it later! If the bling Gods feel you didn't collect enough blings, you're toast.
*Mostly. Black blings kill you, and grey ones are sleeping gas.

Controls

  • Move car: arrow keys
  • Pause: spacebar
  • Mute: m
