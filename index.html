Bling Runner

TL;DR:
Catch the blings!

To advance levels, you must grab enough blings. If you don't, game over.

How many blings, you may ask? As many as you can!

Every bling you grab increases your acceleration. Trust me here.. you'll need it later!

If the bling Gods feel you didn't collect enough blings, you're toast.

Controls

  • Move car: arrow keys
  • Pause: spacebar
  • Mute: m

Blings

Collect these to gain points and level up

Power Ups

  • Super-Speed

    Catching these blings will increase your top speed and acceleration stats!

  • Sleeping Gas

    These cause you to fall asleep and lose control of your vehicle for 3 seconds

  • Bomb

    AVOID THESE at all costs! Bombs will immediately end your run!
